Definitions & Context
Key Concepts Explained
Terms and ideas from the call — plus additional context you need to fully understand the model.
🔗
Plaid
A financial data network that securely connects your bank account to third-party apps. It acts as a bridge — the app never stores your banking credentials.
Mentioned in call
💳
POS (Point of Sale)
The physical or digital system where a purchase transaction occurs. The round-up does NOT happen here — it's a separate, post-transaction charge triggered by Plaid data.
Mentioned in call
🔄
Round-Up Model
A micro-donation mechanism that rounds each purchase to the nearest dollar and redirects the difference. Your $9.60 purchase triggers a $0.40 round-up charge.
Core mechanic
🔔
Transaction Webhooks
Real-time notifications from Plaid when a new transaction posts to the linked account. This is how the app detects purchases and calculates round-ups automatically.
Not mentioned — technical context
🏦
ACH Debit
Automated Clearing House — the electronic funds transfer system used to pull the round-up amount from the user's bank. This is the mechanism behind the separate charge.
Not mentioned — technical context
📊
Unit Economics
At $0.05 per transaction, the platform needs volume to be viable. With 30 transactions/user/month, that's $1.50/user — scale is critical to sustainability.
Not mentioned — business context
Insights & Recommendations
Suggestions for the Product
Actionable ideas surfaced from analyzing the call content.
01

Let Users Choose Their Charity

Allow users to select from a curated list of charities or causes. This increases emotional investment and retention — people are more likely to keep the app if they feel connected to where their money goes.

User Experience
02

Show Real-Time Impact Dashboard

Add a dashboard showing total donated, meals provided, trees planted, etc. Gamification of micro-giving (streaks, milestones) could significantly boost engagement.

User Experience
03

Batch Round-Ups Weekly

Instead of charging per-transaction, batch round-ups into a single weekly ACH debit. This reduces per-transaction fees, lowers bank dispute risk, and feels less intrusive to users.

Technical
04

Add a "Boost" Option

Let users multiply their round-up (2x, 5x, 10x) for specific purchases or causes. This creates a premium tier path without changing the core free model.

Business Model
05

Clarify the Fee in Onboarding

The 5-cent platform fee wasn't clearly framed in the call. During onboarding, show the split transparently — "87.5% to charity, 12.5% keeps us running" builds trust from day one.
